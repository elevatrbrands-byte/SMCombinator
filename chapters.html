From nothing to investible

  1. Week 1–2: Problem & User — choose a problem that matters; define your user.
  2. Week 3–4: Validation — interview scripts, bias traps, insight logs.
  3. Week 5–6: Prototype — lo-fi build, smoke tests, concierge MVP.
  4. Week 7: Business model — unit economics, basic pricing, key assumptions.
  5. Week 8: GTM & Story — ICP, channels, funnel math, narrative arc.
  6. Week 9: Pitch Craft — deck structure, demo, Q&A drills.
  7. Week 10: Submit — chapter showcase; select teams for city qualifiers.
