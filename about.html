About

Mission-driven, student-first.

Grow Dallas’ next generation of founders by making entrepreneurship hands-on, social, and achievable in high school.

Why “D Pitch”?

A tip of the hat to Dallas culture and ambition. D Pitch is an independent student program not affiliated with D Magazine.

Hands-on learning

We believe entrepreneurship is learned by doing. Students run real discovery, ship prototypes, and pitch to judges.

Community first

Chapters operate like startup studios on campus—peer accountability, mentor feedback, and a citywide stage.
