Mission

We’re here to turn DFW ambition into investible startups.

D Pitch is a student-first program built by founders and educators who love Dallas’ maverick, can-do spirit. We run hands-on chapters in private schools, teach the real startup playbook, and showcase the best teams each spring.

What makes D Pitch different

Every chapter runs on action-first habits that mirror the best founder playbooks.

Interviews, experiments, MVPs—every week moves your idea forward. We swap theory for momentum.

Founders, operators, and investors rotate through chapter nights to run workshops and jam on roadblocks.

Chapters advance to a city-wide final before a pro judging panel—students feel the real stakes.

By finals, teams have problem clarity, early traction, and a crisp story. Investors know exactly why it wins.
