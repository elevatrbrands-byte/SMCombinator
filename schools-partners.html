Schools & Partners

Bring D Pitch to your campus.

DFW network of private-school chapters launching Fall 2025.

For schools

  • Advisor guide, club charter, and semester plan.
  • Student-leader playbook with weekly agendas.
  • Safety & safeguards: parental permissions, media release, chaperone checklist.

How it works

  1. Submit the interest form — we send the onboarding kit.
  2. Host an orientation meeting with our team + your advisor.
  3. Launch weekly sessions with starter decks and mentor support.

For sponsors

Why partner with D Pitch? Build the next wave of Dallas founders.

  • Cultivate future talent with real startup reps.
  • Recruit interns early from a diverse pipeline.
  • Support Dallas innovation with tangible outcomes.

Sponsorship tiers

  • Community: $2.5k — logo placement, finals tickets.
  • Chapter: $7.5k — adopt a school, host a workshop.
  • Citywide: $15k — finals booth, stage mention, judging seat.

Partner with us

We’ll tailor sponsorship packages and provide reporting on student outcomes.
