About D Pitch

Mission: Grow Dallas’ next generation of founders by making entrepreneurship hands-on, social, and achievable in high school.

Why “D Pitch”?

D Pitch tips a hat to Dallas culture and ambition. We celebrate the builders shaping what’s next. D Pitch is an independent student program not affiliated with D Magazine.
