FAQ

Short, declarative answers to the questions teams ask most.

Who can join?

Any DFW high-school student (grades 9–12). Homeschoolers and students without chapters can join the city cohort.

Do I need an idea?

No idea yet? Perfect. We teach you how to find one worth building through customer discovery sprints.

Is there a cost?

The program is sponsor-funded. Student participation is free or scholarship-supported.

Time commitment?

Expect 90 minutes per week in chapter plus checkpoints and optional mentor office hours.

Can homeschoolers apply?

Yes—join the city chapter cohort and attend in-person meetups across Dallas.

What if my school doesn’t have a chapter?

Start one! We’ll equip you with the chapter kit, advisor resources, and mentor roster.

Is this after-school?

Yes. Chapters meet weekly on campus or nearby, typically after school or during club blocks.

I’m a freshman—can I join?

Absolutely. Curiosity beats resumes. The track is designed for newcomers.

Do we keep our IP?

Yes, teams keep what they build. Sponsors and mentors sign participation agreements protecting student work.
