Clubs launching Fall 2025

Chapters = your startup studio at school.

Build with friends, learn the fundamentals, get coached by pros. Chapters run as student clubs with weekly meetups and a plug-and-play kit.

Chapter benefits

Licensed D Pitch Curriculum

Plug-and-play slides, worksheets, timers, and facilitation guides keep every session tight.

Mentor Matching

Monthly office hours with founders, investors, and operators who review progress and unblock teams.

Speaker Day Access

Priority Q&A with the headline speakers and exclusive bonus workshops for chapter leads.

Checkpoint Feedback

Rubrics and recorded feedback at key checkpoints to calibrate your problem, solution, and pitch.

Club Ops Kit

School-friendly constitution, role descriptions, promo assets, and onboarding email templates.

From nothing to investible

Weeks 1–2: Problem & User

Pick a problem that matters. Define your user, pain points, and job-to-be-done.

Weeks 3–4: Validation

Interview scripts, bias traps, and insight logs show whether the pain is real and urgent.

Weeks 5–6: Solution & Prototype

Ship lo-fi prototypes, run concierge MVPs, and test assumptions with real users.

Week 7: Business Model

Calculate unit economics, pricing hypotheses, and the earliest proof of willingness to pay.

Week 8: GTM & Story

Lock your ICP, map channels, and script the narrative arc that makes judges lean in.

Week 9: Pitch Craft

Build the deck, rehearse the demo, and drill Q&A with mentors who’ve been on stage.

Week 10: Showcase

Run your chapter showcase, lock city qualifier slots, and prep for the winter gauntlet.
